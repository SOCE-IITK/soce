ABOUT SOCE

SOCE (Society of Civil Engineers, IIT Kanpur) is a student body. It serves as a link between enrolled Civil Engineering students, alumni and professors at IIT Kanpur. This society is an effort to allow free communication and sharing of ideas and information among all.

Events

SOCE organizes various events. It has organized introductory sessions for incoming batches for students in Civil Engineering department. Introductory sessions are primarily focused on introducing the new students to the functions of the society as well as the prospects of choosing civil engineering at IIT Kanpur. SOCE also organizes live sessions to clear doubts and confusions regarding career choices and scopes in civil engineering. SOCE organized series of talks with Alumni to remove all confusion and doubts that student had regarding scopes in civil engineering.
